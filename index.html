01 — About Me

Dancer.
Data Storyteller.

I am Gaeun Choe, a student at Sungkyunkwan University majoring in Dance. I am passionate about exploring the intersection of arts and big data, specifically focusing on K-pop trend analysis.

I love turning complex data into visual stories — finding the rhythm in numbers the same way I find it in movement. Every dataset is a choreography waiting to be interpreted.

My work bridges two worlds that may seem distant but are deeply connected: the expressive language of performance and the analytical precision of data science.
