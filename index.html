01 — About Me

Dancer.
Data Storyteller.

I am Gaeun Choe, a student at Sungkyunkwan University majoring in Dance. I am passionate about exploring the geometric alignment of arts and big data, specifically focusing on K-pop streaming and choreography trend analysis.

I love translating complex dynamic data setups into beautiful visual narratives — discovering the rhythmic structures hidden in numbers much like tracking beats on a stage. Every raw dataset behaves like a unique choreography waiting to find its ideal interpretation.

My artistic vision bridges two worlds that look distinct yet pulse together: the vibrant expressive energy of stage performance and the elegant precision of data science systems.
